垂钓（技能）

杀完了怪，挖完了矿，不如钓钓鱼放松一下心情。垂钓技能可让你在钓鱼时获得奖励，而且这些奖励都是独有的。
你也许会钓到一些更有营养的鱼，如果运气好的话，你甚至可以获得某种宝藏！当你从水中拖出上述物品时，If you're lucky, you may even get some sort of treasure! When you do pull one of these up from the depths, you'll gain a bit of Hauling xp.

The more you level up your Hauling level, the better the loot you'll find!
Some of the fish hiding away down there may be some of the most nutritious meals around..
